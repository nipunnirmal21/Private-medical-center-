Our Services

Providing a wide range of services to meet your every need.

Wheelchair Accessible Entrance

Our facility is fully wheelchair accessible, ensuring safe and convenient entry for patients with mobility needs.

Diagnostic Services

We offer a wide range of diagnostic services including x-rays, ECG, ultrasound, and laboratory blood tests to support accurate medical evaluations.

Pharmacy Service

A fully stocked in-house pharmacy is available for quick and convenient access to prescribed medications.

24/7 OPD

Our Outpatient Department operates 24/7, providing continuous medical consultation and care without the need for prior appointments.

Hospital Wards

Comfortable and clean inpatient wards are available for patients requiring overnight or extended care.

Operation Theatre

We are equipped with a modern, sterile operation theatre designed to support a wide range of surgical procedures.

Departments

Expert medical teams providing comprehensive care across all specialties.

Cardiology

Specialized care for heart health. Our expert cardiologists diagnose and treat a wide range of cardiovascular conditions.

Neurology

Comprehensive diagnosis and treatment for disorders of the nervous system, including the brain, spinal cord, and nerves.

Hepatology

Dedicated to the health of the liver, gallbladder, biliary tree, and pancreas. Our specialists provide expert care for these vital organs.

Pediatrics

Specialized medical care for infants, children, and adolescents. Our team provides compassionate care from birth through young adulthood.

Eye Care

Comprehensive services for vision health, from routine exams and prescription glasses to advanced surgical procedures.
